# Life in Parallel

### Explore the possibilities before making life's biggest decisions.

**Life in Parallel** is an AI-powered decision exploration platform that helps users visualize multiple possible futures based on their important life choices.

Instead of providing generic advice, it creates personalized future paths and compares how different decisions could impact areas such as career growth, wealth, happiness, learning, relationships, freedom, and personal fulfillment.

Powered by **Mesh API with GPT-5**, Life in Parallel transforms uncertainty into an interactive experience where users can explore alternate possibilities and understand the trade-offs behind different choices.

## 💡 The Problem

People make major life decisions about careers, education, relationships, and personal goals while having limited understanding of possible long-term outcomes.

Traditional advice systems provide opinions, but they cannot help people visualize the different paths their choices may create.

---

## 🚀 The Solution

Life in Parallel creates simulated future journeys from a user's decision.

The user provides a life choice, and the AI generates multiple possible futures with different outcomes and trade-offs.

The goal is not to predict the future, but to help users think more clearly about their decisions.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. User enters a major life decision.

2. The decision is processed through Mesh API using GPT-5.

3. AI generates multiple personalized future scenarios.

4. Each future is evaluated across different life dimensions.

5. Users compare possible outcomes and reflect on their choices.
